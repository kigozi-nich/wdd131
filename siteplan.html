Naran Suites and Hotel - Site Plan

Website plan for the Naran Suites and Hotel project

Site Name

Naran Suites and Hotel

This name was chosen as it reflects the luxury and comfort of the hotel, appealing to customers looking for a serene stay.

Site Purpose

The purpose of the website is to provide information about the services and amenities offered by Naran Suites and Hotel, including booking options, customer reviews, and special offers.

Scenarios

  • How can I make a booking at Naran Suites?
  • What amenities are available at the hotel?

Color Schema

Primary Color: #2E3A87 (Navy Blue) - Used for headers, navigation bar, and buttons.

Secondary Color: #F4D03F (Golden Yellow) - Used for call-to-action buttons and accents.

Typography

Font 1: 'Montserrat', sans-serif - Used for headings.

Font 2: 'Open Sans', sans-serif - Used for body text and paragraphs.
