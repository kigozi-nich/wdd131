Our History

Founded in 1985, Naran Suites began as a small boutique hotel with just 15 rooms. Over the decades, we have grown into an international luxury hotel chain while maintaining our commitment to personalized service and exceptional experiences.

Our founder, Nicholas Kigozi, had a vision of creating a space where travelers could feel both the comfort of home and the luxury of a premium hotel experience. That vision guides us to this day as we continue to expand our brand across the globe.

Today, Naran Suites operates in 12 countries with 25 properties, each reflecting the local culture while maintaining our signature approach to hospitality.

Nicholas Kigozi, Founder
